Ud 8.1:3.1–3.4
SN 1.27:2.5–2.6 mentions the cessation of nāmarūpa. In Ud 1.10:12.3–12.6 the verses were spoken when monks asked about the faith of the arahant Bāhiya who just passed away. Ud 8.1 mentions the absence of all form and the formless. All other factors in this inspired utterance describe the end of rebirth from different perspectives. So all three texts are about nibbāna after death.
